Orthodontics

Invisalign

If you have misaligned teeth — one of the most common cosmetic issues — Invisalign can be a fantastic option. It will get you the results you want without having to wear traditional braces. This is preferable for many adults who do not want to wear metal wires and rubber bands in their mouths. When you come in for a consultation, we will scan your mouth using the latest dental technology. This allows us to order your first set of aligners. Once they arrive, you will return to try them on and make sure they fit your teeth perfectly. Then every few weeks, your dentist will order you a new pair as your teeth are slowly moved into proper alignment. Each visit, we will make sure the trays fit comfortably and precisely. Not only are they virtually unnoticeable, they are also removable, so you can eat and drink comfortably without worrying about food particles getting stuck.

Traditional Braces

In addition to clear aligners, we also offer traditional braces. While Invisalign is very popular, traditional braces are preferable in certain cases. When you come in for an orthodontics consultation, we will fully examine your mouth and help you determine the best options for your specific needs.
